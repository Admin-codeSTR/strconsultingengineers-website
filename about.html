About Us

STR Consulting Engineers Ltd provides professional engineering services for bottling, packaging and industrial production lines across Africa and Middle East.

Overview

STR Consulting Engineers Ltd is an OEM-independent industrial engineering company specializing in bottling, packaging, and high-speed production lines.

We provide installation, commissioning, maintenance, automation, and optimization services across multi-OEM production environments including Krones, KHS, Sidel, SIPA, SACMI, and Heuft systems.

Our engineers support beverage, food, and manufacturing facilities with technical expertise focused on improving efficiency, reducing downtime, and maximizing equipment lifecycle.
